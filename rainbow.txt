We See Leaves of Unicorns and Ponies
 
We see orange unicorns playing with Ponies,
     We see brown,
We see leaves,
     On the ground there is a Pony.
 
We see yellow unicorns, 
     We see red,
We see leaves,
     Above our head are lots of unicorns that look like Ponies.
     ~Author  is an Unknown Unicorn
